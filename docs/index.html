Who this is for

A path for the curious beginner — held honestly

★ Taught faithfully

You'll learn the real tradition: Robert Monroe's Focus states, Robert Bruce's rope technique and energy work, William Buhlman's intention-led approach — the vibrational state, the silver cord, the exit, the navigation. Nothing watered down.

◎ Grounded throughout

And you'll learn what's happening in the body and brain: hypnagogia, REM atonia, sleep paralysis, and the temporoparietal junction. We keep experiential, evidenced, and interpretive claims clearly apart — always.

⚠ Before you begin

This is a practice of attention and sleep states, not medicine, and it makes no claims of verified travel. Fear can feel intense but is not dangerous. If you have a history of psychosis or schizophrenia-spectrum conditions, dissociative disorders, or severe anxiety or panic, talk to a professional before intensive practice. Module 00 covers safety in full.

Astral Projection · Zero → Hero

This course teaches astral projection / out-of-body experience as a practice of attention and sleep states. It is not medical or psychological treatment and makes no claims that you travel to real, externally verifiable places. Practitioner reports are treated as experiential; the science is presented as evidenced; metaphysical framing is presented as interpretive — and these are never blurred. If intensive practice causes persistent distress, stop and seek support.
